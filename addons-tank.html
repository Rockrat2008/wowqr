What are Add-Ons?

Add-Ons are plug-ins that can enhance your game experience and are not required to play World of Warcraft. I did not use any add-ons for about the first year myself. You can download and install add-ons manually, but by far the easiest way is to use the WowUp World of Warcraft AddOn Updater. You can search for Add-Ons, download and install Add-Ons, and update all your Add-Ons all from one simple interface.

In this "Tank Add-Ons" section I will recommend add-ons that I find useful for the tank specialization that just make your gameplay experience better. I will give a brief explanation of the add-on so you can decide if you want to try it or not.

Omen Threat Meter

This add-on is will allow you to see the amount of threat you have on a monster or mob of monsters. As the tank it is your responsibility to hold the threat so the monsters attack you instead of your DPS party members and healer(s). You can see if other members are pulling the threat of of you and try to taunt, or focus your attacks on mosters you are losing threat on.

Threat Plates

This is another add-on for monitoring threats, but it also puts a red exclamation point over the head of monsters when questing so you know exactly what monsters you're looking to kill at any time. I prefer Omen (above) for the threat meter but this for questing.
